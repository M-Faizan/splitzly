Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Splitzly ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Splitzly, we collect:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign up
  • Expense data — descriptions, amounts, categories, and dates you enter
  • Profile photo — if you choose to upload one
  • Group and friend data — group names, member lists, and friend connections you create
  • Receipt images — photos you submit for AI scanning (processed and discarded, not stored)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Splitzly service
  • To calculate and display expense splits and balances
  • To send messages between users within the app
  • To improve the app experience

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

4. Receipt Scanning (AI Feature)

When you scan a receipt, the image is sent to an AI service to extract the amount and items. The image is processed in real time and is not permanently stored by us or the AI provider.

5. Sharing Your Information

Expense and balance information is shared with the friends and group members you choose to add in the app. We do not share your data with any other third parties except as required to operate the service (e.g. our database provider).

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your account by contacting us at privacy@splitzly.app, after which all your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Splitzly is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at privacy@splitzly.app to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app. Continued use of Splitzly after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@splitzly.app
